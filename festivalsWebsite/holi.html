Holi

Holi is an ancient Hindu festival celebrating the arrival of spring, the victory of good over evil, and love. It is celebrated over two days with rituals like the "Holika Dahan" bonfire and the main "Rangwali Holi" which involves playing with vibrant colors, singing, and dancing. It is also a time for forgiveness, making up with others, and sharing festive foods.
